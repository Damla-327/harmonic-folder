Main Theme: This site will highlight the obvious blemish and concolence of reality, in a way that will gamify materiality in a purely expressive and child-like way. In the hopes of indicating the paradox and the chaotic complexities of life, I will try to coin to venture etherealness and whimsicality into the gates of retro illustrations, acid posters, retro, anti-design, 90 nostalgia, and frasurbane art. This magazine site will contain visuals of mostly digital collage-like designs for the reason and demand of real touch. It will always have contrasting colors, sharp graphics, and static typographic layouts to give the site movements and dimensions. Artists have used mirrors to look outward, symbolizing sight and truth. Early modern personifications of truth often incorporated mirrors and scales. Initially, this magazine will serve as an act of mirroring to viewers. The complexities of life as stated before are divided into two gerenal groups; one as wastfull habits as for the second one it sheds light on the presence of nature and the sipirituality it holds. In the wastfull habits section, I moved from the example of fabric waste and made multiple versions to indicate its rapid negativity on the planet. In the sipurituality of nature section, where I wanted shed light on the effort to reflect the continous and eternal growth of nature. This site is more of an artsy site rather than educational, so diverse and different perspectives and opinions will always be supported with the designs interprated. The reason my designs have such a graphic and fun tone is to gamify the seriousness and the greatness of this blemish concerning nature. I tried to indicate these values, with the conjunction of fused colors from the relation and regions that give a base to flat and angular surfaces with unconstructive structures by the use of ripped papers from old magazines. I tried to reveal the relations between papers and digital positioning, inorder to bring the effort of existence together at a literary point.
